THE EXPERIENCE

Calling all Offliners who a) love wine, b) are looking to meet new people, and/or c) want to level up your average weeknight plans. Cause it's time to wine down with the ultimate wine-tasting experience complete with charcuterie boards + mingling, and the chance to vibe on their heated outdoor patio.

Included in your $25 ticket 🎫:

🍷 a self-guided wine tasting with 3 kinds of wines

🧀 a personal charcuterie board (add-ons available to purchase)

Lottery closes: 3PM, Jan 27. Winners notified via email before: 6PM, Jan 27

WHY WE 💙'EM

________________________________________

The Raleigh Wine Shop- located in a newly renovated historic building in the Old East district of DTR-is one of our fave spots to grab a glass of pinot. This stunning space has a full wine bar for in-house tastings + classes, plenty of indoor and outdoor seating for mixin' and minglin', and dozens of shelves filled with curated collections of hand-selected wines.

Don't know about y'all, but this may just be our wine in a
million 😅
